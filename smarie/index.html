About

Aeriel is a high red pineapple conure who is super cute and awesome. I made this website because I had nothing better to do and love her so much!

Aeriel likes to do crazy flips, eat salad, and shred popsicle sticks. She also likes electronics, glasses, and jumping up to the table. Her wings have not yet grown in so she cannot fly. Aeriel's birthday is on February 26th, 2021.
