Baden Optical is a family owned and operated business.

We carry major brand designers and sunglasses as well as contact lenses from major companies. We are affiliated with Essilor, the worlds largest manufacturer and is a company driven by innovation - always ahead of the latest technology. It is because of their guarantee of high quality and service that we are able to offer warantees on most of the lenses we sell.

With over 20 years of sales experience, we are dedicated to providing the best in customer service and ensure that our customers are completely satisfied with their experience. With the personal experience and quality at prices you would expect from a small town boutique... it's worth the drive to Baden!
