yt - Plays YouTube videos and audio.
Usage: yt (video/audio) (volume <percent>) <search term>

Plays the first result for the search term found on YouTube.

EXAMPLES
    yt
	Opens the guided player. (Good for beginners)
    yt video ModMonster
	Plays the first result for the search "ModMonster" as a video
    yt volume 40 audio ModMonster
	Plays the first result for the search "ModMonster" at 40% volume, with audio only.
